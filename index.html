About Me

Hi, my name is Dominique Cruz, and I am an aspiring digital artist. I attended CAWD during junior year with Mr. Cronin at the CTE. I learned all about Blender, my first experience with 3d modeling. After learning about the basic modeling skills, I decided to delve deeper and learned about sculpting. While I have had a lot of experience in coding and other fields while at CTE, I have gravitated towards sculpting. I would love to be able to gain more experience in digital sculpting as well as traditional. I hope to be able to learn more about this in college and hopefully make a career out of it.
